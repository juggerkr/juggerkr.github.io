South Korea's first Jugger team!

We are the first team in history to play Jugger in South Korea, made up of high schoolers trying to learn and grow the sport. We train weekly in our school's field.

What is Jugger?

Jugger is a 5v5 team sport blending fencing, rugby, and capture-the-flag.
